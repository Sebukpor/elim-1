Important to Know

Booking Policy

We want your experience to be seamless. Please read our booking terms.

📅

Plan Ahead

Appointments should be booked 5–7 days in advance, preferably earlier, to secure your preferred date and time.

💳

Booking Fee

A booking fee is required to secure your appointment. This fee is fully adjustable toward your final service cost.

⚡

Emergency Bookings

Bookings made 3 days or less before your appointment attract an additional late booking fee, paid together with the booking fee.

🔄

Cancellations

Cancellations more than 48 hours before your appointment are eligible for a refund. Within 48 hours — non-refundable.

🚫

No-Shows

Clients who do not attend their appointment without notice automatically forfeit their booking fee.

📝

Rescheduling

Appointments may be rescheduled once at no cost outside the 48-hour window. Rescheduling after a late cancellation or no-show requires a new booking fee.
